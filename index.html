What we offer

We provide wide range of services in our metering service

DATA & COMMUNICATIONS

Maxwell 1.0 is a fully cellular connected smart meter that uses 3G/4G networks to send energy data and notifications directly to our backend & analytics services. This cellular connection is free, and enables consumers and utilities to access their meter data remotely, regardless of location or time of day. With the new wireless capability, consumers can get automatic payment confirmations and low balance notifications.
